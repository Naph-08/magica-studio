02 Payments
All projects require a non-negotiable 50% deposit before work begins. The remaining 50% is due upon delivery of final assets — before source files are released. Written Client approval is required before the Studio proceeds to the next project phase. Once approval is given, revisions to the approved stage incur additional charges. If the Client does not respond within 7 calendar days of a delivery despite follow-up, the delivery is considered approved.
05 Timelines & Delivery
Timelines begin from: (a) deposit received and cleared, AND (b) all required Client materials received. Delays caused by late client content, slow approval responses, or unclear feedback are not the Studio's responsibility. Standard delivery: Starter 48–72hrs, Growth 3–5 working days, Premium 5–7 working days. Working days are Monday to Friday, excluding Kenyan public holidays.
06 Project Abandonment
If a Client becomes unresponsive for more than 21 consecutive calendar days despite two follow-up attempts, the project is placed on hold. Projects on hold for more than 60 days without response will be formally closed. The deposit is retained. A new deposit and brief are required to restart.
